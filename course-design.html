IRAS Syllabus Breakdown

A comprehensive technical roadmap designed to transform you into an AI-Native Full Stack Architect.

Module 1

Civil Engineering Application

Focus: Drawing & Construction Fundamentals
  • Engineering Drawing: Mastering Scales, Units, and Unit Conversion
  • Site Mastery: Vasthu, Site Measuring, and Manual Presentation
  • Construction: Setting Out, Site Training, and Structural Details
  • Practical details from Setting Out to Coping
Module 2

Industry Standard Softwares

Focus: AutoCAD, Revit & BIM Workflow
  • AutoCAD: Plan, Elevation, Section, and Roof/Stair Designing
  • Legal Drawings: Sancion Drawing and Completion Drawings
  • Revit Architecture: Project Drafting, Model View, and Rendering
